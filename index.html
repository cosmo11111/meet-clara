FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Does Clara connect to my bank account?
+
No. Clara never connects to your bank. You export a PDF statement from your bank's app and upload it — that's it. No login credentials, no open banking, no ongoing access.
Is my financial data stored?
+
Your PDF is never stored — it's processed in memory and discarded immediately. If you save a report, only the summary data (category totals, metrics) is stored, encrypted, on secure servers.
Which banks does Clara work with?
+
Clara works with any Australian bank that lets you export a PDF statement — including ANZ, CommBank, Westpac, NAB, ING, Macquarie, and most others.
Can I cancel my subscription?
+
Yes, anytime. No lock-in periods, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your settings page and you won't be charged again.
What does beta mean for me?
+
Clara is in early access. The core product is fully working — you can upload statements, get AI categorisation, and save reports right now. As a beta user you get access to all features at current prices, and we'll give you plenty of notice before anything changes.
